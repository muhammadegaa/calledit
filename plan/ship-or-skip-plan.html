1 · Flows (the first one decides the hackathon)

FIRST VISIT — a judge or voter, single session, must feel complete

Land→ “Yesterday, 3,012 people judged 5 launches. Here's what reality said.” instant reveal payoff→ “Your turn — today's 5”→ judge 5 launches (30s each)→ instant provisional read: “you vs the crowd” + vs-the-bot→ share card + “reality scores you tomorrow 9:00”

Key design move: the unresolved 24h bet is the last beat, never the first. First visit opens with yesterday's resolved round (real data, real outcome) so the loop is felt immediately. Crowd % + bot comparison give same-session feedback before reality's verdict.

RETURNING VISIT (the ritual)

Land→ REVEAL: yesterday's calls scored, streak updated, calibration moves→ today's round→ share / leaderboard

2 · Screens

S1 · Judge screen — launch card (live screenshot of real landing page, name, tagline, source badge, hours-since-launch), 30s timer ring, SHIP/SKIP buttons, round progress 3/5. Mock approved by Ega (mockups/ship-or-skip.html).
S2 · Reveal screen — per-launch verdict rows (✓/✗ + what actually happened: “#4 on PH”, “died at 3 points”), one editorial line per reveal, streak flame, calibration deltas animating.
S3 · Calibration profile — overall %, per-category bars (devtools / AI / consumer / b2b), n calls, percentile vs crowd. THE share object.
S4 · Share card — OG-image endpoint: grid of today's calls (🟩🟥 wordle-grammar), calibration %, “Can you read a launch? shipskip.app”.

Sources: HN Algolia (free, keyless) for Show HN; PH GraphQL v2 (token, non-commercial OK) for PH launches. Screenshot of each landing page captured at ingest (our own headless shot, cached).

Supabase tables: launches (id, source, url, title, tagline, shot_url, launched_at, category), rounds (date, launch_ids[5]), calls (anon_id, launch_id, call, ts), resolutions (launch_id, outcome, peak_rank, points, resolved_at).

Crons (Vercel): 09:00 ingest — pick 5 judgeable launches ~4-8h old; 09:00+24h resolve — fetch final rank/points, score all calls, precompute crowd stats.

Identity: anon_id in localStorage. No login anywhere.

4 · Decision tree — open spec questions (P2: answered before code)

D1 · Ground truth. Both sources, two leagues (HN = organic league, PH = launch-machine league) ⟶ recommended: the contrast is content · or HN-only (cleanest resolution) · or PH-only.

Resolution defs: HN SHIP = reaches front page (rank ≤ 30) within 24h. PH SHIP = top 5 of its day. Both fully computable from APIs.

D2 · The bot. Claude makes its own 5 calls daily; players see “you vs crowd vs the bot”. In v1 (needs API credits, ~1 call/day = pennies) ⟶ recommended · or post-hackathon.
D3 · Day boundary. Rounds cut at 09:00 WIB (your demo timezone) · 09:00 user-local (complex) · 00:00 PT (PH-native). ⟶ recommended: fixed UTC+7 morning, shown as “today's round” everywhere.
D4 · Name & domain. Ship/Skip (shipskip.app?) · The Front Page · Called It · other. Affects share-card copy, buy domain day 1.
